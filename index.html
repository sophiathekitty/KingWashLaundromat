About King Wash

Located in the heart of Grindstone, King Wash Laundromat has been serving the community for generations. From washing your everyday essentials to entertaining you with our backroom arcade, we bring cleanliness and fun to your busy life!

Our Services

  • High-efficiency washing machines
  • Large-capacity dryers
  • Soap and supplies vending
  • Comfortable seating area
  • Self-service and drop-off options

Got time to spare? Check out our arcade in the back for a spin on nostalgia!
